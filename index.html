Sidewalk Widths

During 2020, COVID-19 made urban dwellers acutely aware of how the width of sidewalks impacted their ability to maintain social distance. This prompted cartographers from across the world to map the sidewalk widths in their cities. This website compiles the maps from those grassroots efforts.
